Pradip Swarnakar

Associate Professor


Pradip Swarnakar is an Associate Professor of Sociology at the Department of Humanities and Social Sciences, Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur, India. Dr. Swarnakar is an environmental sociologist specialized in climate change policy, social network analysis, and sustainability transition. He obtained his doctoral degree from IIT Kanpur in 2008. Before joining IIT Kanpur, he was an associate professor at ABV-Indian Institute of Information Technology and Management Gwalior. He has been a visiting scholar at the Climate Justice Research Centre, University of Technology Sydney, Australia the Department of Environmental Studies, University of San Francisco, USA, the Department of Social Research, University of Helsinki, Finland, and the Department of Urban and Environmental Sociology, Helmholtz Centre for Environmental Research GmbH-UFZ, Germany. He was a recipient of the Fulbright-Nehru Academic & Professional Excellence Award (Lecturing & Research), Kone Foundation senior researcher grant, and Deutsche Forschungsgemeinschaft bilateral grant. His work has been published in the British Journal of Sociology, Current Sociology, Socius, Environmental Science and Policy, and Economic and Political Weekly. He is co-editor of the book Bottom-up Approaches in Governance and Adaptation for Sustainable Development: Case Studies from India and Bangladesh (SAGE Publications, 2017).
